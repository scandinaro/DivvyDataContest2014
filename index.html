See the City with Divvy

Welcome, traveler!

On this trip, you'll explore Chicago, the City in a Garden. Although the L has been called a marvel of engineering, bicycles allow an intimate view of the city. In the past few years, Chicago has implemented more bicycle lanes, some of which are protected, just in time for your visit. Splendid! This report will outline information from the bicycle sharing program, Divvy.

Before you get started, please notice a set of filters above. These will guide you through most of the information listed below. Simply select the variables you'd like to view, and click the filter button. Now you're ready to get rolling!
